OpptyCon for Board Members

For boards that need signal, not fifty pages of dashboards.

Most board readouts are after-the-fact reporting wrapped in narrative. OpptyCon gives board members five minutes of operating signal — quarterly waterfall, biggest leading-indicator miss, attainment posture, defensibility of the load-bearing assumptions.

Launch OpptyCon
What You're Up Against

Reporting theater.

Quarterly board decks: 40 pages of bar charts and "we're confident about Q3." A revenue miss at quarter-end that nobody saw coming three weeks earlier. A management team that brings answers without showing the math.

You don't need more dashboards. You need leading indicators with verdicts attached.

Most board members read on phones at midnight. The deck that's optimized for the meeting is the wrong deck. What's needed is a stable surface with a small number of high-signal numbers and one clear verdict on each.

What OpptyCon Does

How OpptyCon helps board members.

Render the quarterly waterfall on one screen — closing deals, SQOs needed, MQLs needed across 8 quarters, phase-shifted
Surface the single biggest leading-indicator miss as a verdict — burn, capacity gap, funnel grade, coverage health, CAC payback — ranked by severity
Show implied AE attainment realism — if the plan requires the average AE to perform at 120%, that's a structural problem, not a coaching problem
Frame S&M investment posture against the growth-vs-burn band — is the company underinvesting given the target, or burning toward death?
Audit the defensibility of the six load-bearing assumptions — target ARR, deal size, conversion, capacity, NRR, CAC payback — with each row's source visible
Outcomes

What changes for board members.

Five minutes of signal instead of fifty of dashboards.

Leading indicators, not lagging

Pipeline coverage, attainment realism, funnel grade — the numbers that predict the quarter, not the ones that record it after it's over.

One biggest miss, ranked

Instead of 40 charts, the single highest-priority signal with a recommended move and a link to where it lives. Read in 30 seconds.

Defensibility panel

For each load-bearing input, see what it is and where it came from. Inputs the team can't source back to recent data are the ones to challenge.

The growth-vs-burn read

Is the company under-investing given the target, or burning toward death? S&M as % of revenue with explicit band verdict.

Better questions in the meeting

Walk into the board meeting knowing where the plan is fragile. Ask the management team about the specific gap, not the headline number.

Less reporting theater

The management team prepares to defend the model, not to perform certainty. The honest conversation gets shorter, sharper, more useful.

What You Can Finally Control

With OpptyCon, board members can finally:

Read GTM health in five minutes on a phone
Spot the biggest leading-indicator miss before the management team raises it
Challenge load-bearing assumptions with the source visible per row
Tell underinvestment from burn-toward-death with one number, not a debate
Run a board meeting on signal, not theater

Read the company in five minutes.

30-minute briefing. We'll show your board view with the company's actual numbers.
